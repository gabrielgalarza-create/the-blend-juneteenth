The vision

Coffee, R&B classics, and community — a Juneteenth day party.

The Blend is Over The Top's Juneteenth-weekend social, presented by Hennessy. A daytime party built around your R&B classics, your coffee of choice, and the people who make Oakland move.

Café lattes and espresso martini towers, a Hennessy VIP lounge, live DJ sets, and a $300 sing-along — one afternoon at Hella Bees, dressed in coffee tones.
